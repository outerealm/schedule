💙 Harry's Daily Care Guide

Harry is 99 and fiercely independent — he may say he doesn't need help, but small things throughout the day make a big difference. Here's what to keep in mind when you visit.

🌅
Morning
7:00 – 9:00 AM
  • Give medication with breakfast
  • Make breakfast and sit with him
  • Check bed pad & sheets — change if soiled
  • Empty the basin if needed
  • Offer water or oat milk
☀️
Midday / Lunch
2:00 – 4:00 PM
  • Prepare lunch and keep him company
  • Offer snacks or drinks throughout
  • Help get water or oat milk as needed
  • Check in on how he's feeling
🌇
Evening
Around 6:00 PM
  • Light dinner or snack by 6 PM
  • After 6 PM — liquids only. Eating later upsets his digestion and makes him unwell overnight
  • Water, oat milk, or broth are fine
🌙
Bedtime
7:00 – 9:00 PM
  • Help him change into a clean, dry diaper for overnight absorption
  • Apply Vaseline to his private area and upper legs to prevent rashes or infection
  • Make sure he's comfortable and settled
